Detailed Description

Implementation of the armored walking enemy functionality.

Walking enemy with enhanced armor for increased durability.

Inherits from WalkingEnemy and represents an armored variant that has increased damage resistance. Behaves identically to a regular walking enemy but takes reduced damage due to armor.
