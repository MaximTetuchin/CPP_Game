Detailed Description

Implementation of the minigun weapon functionality.

Rapid-fire minigun weapon with high rate of fire but lower damage per bullet.

Inherits from Weapon and implements minigun-specific behavior including very high firing rate, multiple bullets per shot, and low damage per bullet.
