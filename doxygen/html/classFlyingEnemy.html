Detailed Description

Implementation of the flying enemy functionality.

Airborne enemy that flies in patterns and ignores ground collision.

Inherits from Enemy and represents a flying enemy that moves through the air without being affected by gravity. Can fly in various patterns and poses a different threat than ground-based enemies.
