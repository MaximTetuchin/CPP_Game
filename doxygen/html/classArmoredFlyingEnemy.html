Detailed Description

Implementation of the armored flying enemy functionality.

Flying enemy with enhanced armor for increased durability.

Inherits from FlyingEnemy and represents an armored variant that has increased damage resistance. Behaves identically to a regular flying enemy but takes reduced damage due to armor.
